The MC HCK (pronounced: "McHack" [m\aelig{}k h\aelig{}k]) is a
/small/, /cheap/, and /versatile/ ARM Cortex-M4 based microcontroller
platform.

The MC HCK is entirely /open source/ and created by the community,
from geeks, for geeks.
